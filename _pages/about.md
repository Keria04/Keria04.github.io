I am currently an undergraduate student at **South China University of Technology (SCUT)**, pursuing a dual degree in **Software Engineering** and **Business Administration**. 

My research interests primarily lie in the fields of **Computer Vision** and **Multimodal Learning**, with a particular focus on **Visual Grounding**. I am passionate about bridging the gap between natural language and visual perception through efficient neural architectures.

# Research Interests
* **Visual Grounding:** Improving localization precision in complex scenes.
* **Multimodal Models:** Exploring pre-trained backbones (like BEiT-3) for vision-language tasks.
* **Software Engineering:** Applying robust engineering principles to deep learning workflows.
